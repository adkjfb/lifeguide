Virginia Opossum

Lifespan: 1-3 years in the wild, up to 7 years in captivity

Top speed: 4 mph

Weight: 4-13 lbs

Diet: Small vertebrates, eggs, insects, fruits

Predators: Red foxes, owls, hawks

Range: Central America to Southern Canada, except the rocky mountain region

Conservation Status: Least concern

Fun Fact: Virginia opossums have the most teeth of any North American land mammal, with 50 teeth.


Fun Fact: Virginia opossums are highly resistant to rabies because of their low body temperature, and are highly susceptible to frostbite.
