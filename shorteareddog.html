Short-Eared Dog

Lifespan: 9-11 years in captivity, poorly understood in the wild. Most captive short-eared dogs do not survive a full year.

Top speed: Unknown

Weight: 19-22 lbs

Diet: Small mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians, fish, insects, occasionally fruit

Predators: Jaguars, pumas, boa constrictors, ocelots

Range: Amazon Basin

Conservation Status: Near threatened

Fun Fact: Short-eared dogs are also known as ghost dogs because they are very elusive and little is known about them.


Fun Fact: Short-eared dogs are one of the two species of canid found in the Amazon rainforest, and is the only species endemic to the Amazon, meaning they are found nowhere else.
