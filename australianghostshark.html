Australian Ghostshark

Lifespan: 15 years

Top speed: Unknown

Weight: 6.6-8.8 lbs

Diet: Small fish, invertebrates

Predators: Larger fish, including the broadnose sevengill shark

Range: Off the coast of southern Australia

Conservation Status: Least concern

Fun Fact: Australian ghostsharks have a venomous dorsal spine, but it is not enough to harm humans.


Fun Fact: Australian ghost sharks are not sharks, and only have one gill slit on either side of its body.
