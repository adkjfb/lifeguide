Pointed-Headed Caecilian

Lifespan: 4-5 years

Top speed: Unknown

Weight: 15-25 grams

Diet: Small invertabrates, including ants and earthworms

Predators: Birds, fish, tenrecs, amphibians, snakes

Range: Primarily Thailand and Laos

Conservation Status: Data Deficient

Fun Fact: Pointed-headed Caecilians are fossorial, meaning they spend much of their life underground.


Fun Fact: Their genus name, ichthyophis, means "fish snake".
